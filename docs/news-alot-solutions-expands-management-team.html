ALOT Solutions Expands Management Team

30th of January, 2020

ALOT Solutions have today announced two senior hires - Chief Operations Officer Marek Mahdal and Chief Risk Officer Dr Markus Stricker. These appointments follow the ALOT Solutions rebrand last month with more announcements coming soon as CEO Mike Carruthers positions the business to provide prize cover, technology and content to a wider range of customers.

Marek brings 20 years of experience from his previous C-level positions at a number of technology, e-commerce, financial services and lottery businesses - these included Kootac and Rockchain. He is a senior digital leader, product development and operations specialist and will be overseeing ALOT Solution’s business operations - ensuring that effective operational control and reporting procedures are in place and all systems can scale with our partners’ requirements.

Markus joins ALOT Solutions as Chief Risk Officer. He brings with him a huge wealth of experience including over 20 years in the insurance industry. Markus’ career includes being CEO of Twelve Capital – an insurance linked securities firm, Managing Director of Aon Resolution and a senior lecturer of the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology. Markus holds a PhD in Math and taught Mathematics and Computer Science in Chicago and Zurich. He is responsible for creating re/insurance solutions for technology based businesses and oversees ALOT Solution’s risk and prize cover facilities.

Mike further commented: “I am proud to announce Markus and Marek are joining ALOT Solutions’ management team as we expand our ambitions and capabilities. Attracting talent of such high calibre strengthens ALOT Solutions ability to power innovation in the lottery, gaming and prize promotion sectors to benefit players, operators and their good causes.”
